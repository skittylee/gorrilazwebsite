BEST GORILLAZ ALBUMS

What is Gorillaz?

Gorillaz is a virtual band that was formed by former Blur front man Daimon Albarn and comic book artist Jamie Hewlett. Four members where created for the band; 2D (keyboard and vocalist), Murdoc (leader and bass), Russel (drums) and Noodle (guitar). The Gorillaz are known for branching out different genres and styles of music along with collaborating with well over 100 different artists.

Albums

Gorillaz released there first studio self titled album in 2001. Since then they have released eight other studio albums. Each album explores a different genre and exploration of music. Here I wanted to list best of what they have to offer including a highlight song from each album.
